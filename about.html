Our Story

How we came to be,

Family run and owned, JJ's Snack Shack was first brought into this world in 2009 by John and Joanne Edgley. Joanne drove past the trailer everyday to get to her job and John was unemployed and loved to cook so they bought the trailer. It was a match made in heaven.

JJ's is open 7 days a week and serves up a variety of food ranging from ten different poutines and burgers and chicken wings all served with a smile. You may be thinking, "where have I seen them before..", JJ's Snack Shack is a food vendor at Kemptville and Brockville Ribfest and was named one of the best chipwagon's in Ontario in Cottage Life Magazines top 9 chipwagon's in Ontario article.
